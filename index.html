Recovery Protocols

Coleus Rejuvenation

If Vittorio looks vine-like, perform a Pinch. Cut stems back to just above a leaf node. This triggers dormant buds, creating a lush habit.

Status: Active Pruning

Monstera Acclimation

Gino's small leaves without holes indicate light starvation. Gradually move closer to a window over 7 days. Tie to a moss pole for support.

Status: Light Transition

Humidity Triage

Tito, Regina, and Diva suffer crispy edges in dry air. Group them together, use pebble trays, and run a localized humidifier.

Status: Environmental
